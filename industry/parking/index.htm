Smarter Parking, Safer Spaces

Managing modern parking environments requires more than just barriers and tickets. Aeronix brings AI-powered intelligence to detect available spots, prevent unauthorized access, and monitor behaviour for safety and efficiency. Whether it’s a mall, airport, or private lot, our system helps optimize space utilization, streamline operations, and enhance user experience.

Unlocking Value with AI

Maximize Parking Efficiency

Use AI to track real-time occupancy of parking bays. Identify underused areas and peak usage trends to optimize space allocation. Provide live availability updates to drivers to reduce search time.

Frictionless and Secure Entry

Automate entry and exit with license plate recognition and access rules. Detect tailgating or misuse of gates. Enable secure, ticketless access for staff, tenants, or subscribers.

Prevent Damage Before It Happens

Monitor for suspicious behaviour, break-ins, or loitering in parking zones. Get instant alerts for security threats before damage or loss occurs. Improve safety without full-time guards.

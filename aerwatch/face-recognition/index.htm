Advanced Face Recognition with AerWatch®

AerWatch® makes it easy to recognize and track faces across your spaces in real-time. From identifying staff and VIPs to investigating incidents, everything is instant and intuitive.

Face Recognition by Group

Categorize faces into predefined groups such as staff, VIPs, blacklists, or frequent visitors. Get real-time notifications when someone of interest is detected and automate your security and engagement workflows.

Face Trail: Analyze Movement Journeys

Understand how individuals move across zones over time. With Face Trail, track their journey from entry to exit, dwell times, and zone transitions, ideal for operational analysis and behavioral insights.

Face Recall: Search by Image

Upload or select a face to instantly retrieve all recorded sightings of that individual across your site. Face Recall is your instant archive search engine, perfect for incident reviews, investigations, or visitor audits.

Face Recognition Mustering

Automatically detect who is present in a muster area using facial recognition, no manual checklists needed. Ideal for emergency roll calls, evacuations, and compliance reporting. Improve speed, accuracy, and accountability during critical events.

Face Recognition People Counting

Go beyond generic counts, AerWatch recognizes and deduplicates individual faces for truly accurate people counting. Prevent double-counting of the same person re-entering zones and gain better insights into unique traffic. Perfect for occupancy tracking, footfall analytics, and staff monitoring.

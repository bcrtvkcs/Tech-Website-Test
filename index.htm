VENUES

Iconic Performing Arts Venue

To manage crowd safety during peak performances, this iconic waterfront venue leverages AerWatch real-time people counting and zone-based alerts to reduce congestion at key entry points. Trespassing detection adds a layer of security by monitoring access to back-of-house and restricted areas.

RETAIL

Mirvac

Mirvac uses AerWatch counting suite with the secure AerWatch.io portal to monitor visitor flow across its shopping centres. With real-time occupancy insights and historical trends, the operations team can make faster decisions on staffing, layout, and campaign effectiveness.

TOURISM

Australian Museum

The Australian Museum uses AerWatch people counting and dwell time analytics to understand how guests engage with exhibits. Trespassing detection adds an extra layer of security by monitoring access to staff-only and sensitive areas.

TOURISM

Australian National Maritime Museum

The Australian National Maritime Museum uses AerWatch counting suite with the secure AerWatch.io portal to track visitor flow across its indoor and outdoor spaces. Real-time and historical data support decisions on exhibition planning, staffing, and event logistics.

AVIATION

Brisbane Airport Corporation

Brisbane Airport leverages AerWatch to monitor Persons and Vehicles of Interest, automatically count vehicles moving through security-controlled areas, and detect security breaches such as entry through exit lanes. These insights help enhance situational awareness and support critical decision-making across airport operations.

AVIATION

Melbourne Airport

Melbourne Airport leverages AerWatch to detect Persons and Vehicles of Interest, monitor vehicle flow through access-controlled zones, and identify security breaches like reverse movement through exit points. These tools provide real-time visibility and help streamline both airside and landside operations.

AerAccess® Iris Recognition

Fast Iris & Face Capture Unit

AerAccess® is the world’s first self-levelling, touchless, plug-and-play iris and face capture platform. It eliminates the “human familiarity factor” from traditional iris scanning by automating the entire process.

Aeronix’s intelligent system locates the user’s eyes and automatically positions the robotic scanner for a perfect capture — all in under one second. Designed for high-volume environments, it ensures fast, accurate, and reliable identification.
